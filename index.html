I'm a person who wanted to be a Physicist as a kid.
And cause of that I have a really good mathematics background. Which helps me use math most of the times in my Deep Learning Projects

So I used to learn, write and make sense of physics equations and I even tried to make my own discoveries.

I started coding when I was in 5th or 6th standard in school. Then I got busy exploring every field of science, doing projects like Mini Jet Engine, Arduino-powered robots, and made my first RL powered crawling robot during 12th standard.

Then I got into SCT college, made an AI-Robotics Team called Eurekabotics (then called Muskum Pillerum). I did many cool projects at college and got my first internship in first year itself.

I worked at a robotics company in my 3rd year, then as an intern at Pathor for 2 months in my final year. During the same time, I became a founding team member of Advop. Then I worked at Pathor as a full-time employee for 2 months before resigning to continue my contributions to Advop.

Currently, I am working as an Associate Software Engineer at H&R Block as a Adobe Target and Analytics Engineer.
